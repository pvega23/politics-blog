Every single American paycheck contains a deduction for Social Security. For millions, it's not just a line item; it's a foundational pillar of their retirement security, disability support, and survivor benefits. But what if that pillar isn't as solid as it seems?

For decades, the solvency of Social Security has been a recurring topic in Washington. Forecasts predict that without changes, the program will be unable to pay 100% of promised benefits within the next decade.
