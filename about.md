## About Pulse Politics

Politics affects everything — your paycheck, your healthcare, your kids' schools, even the price of groceries. But most political coverage is either too partisan to trust or too wonky to understand.

Pulse Politics is different. We cut through the noise to explain what's actually happening in Washington and why it matters to **you**.

### What We Cover

- **Breaking News** — Real-time analysis of the stories shaping the nation
- **Policy** — What new laws and regulations actually mean for your daily life
- **Opinion** — Honest, centrist takes that don't follow a party line
- **Economy** — How political decisions impact jobs, prices, and your wallet
- **Foreign Affairs** — America's role in the world, explained simply
- **Elections** — Nonpartisan coverage of races, polls, and what to watch for

### Our Approach

We're not left. We're not right. We're interested in the **truth** and in explaining how political decisions affect real people.

Every article asks one core question: *What does this mean for everyday Americans?*

No spin. No partisan cheerleading. Just clear-eyed analysis you can actually use.
